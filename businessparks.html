Crawfordsville & Montgomery County
Chamber of Commerce

Business Parks

There are currently two business parks located in the Crawfordsville/Montgomery County area. One is designed for smaller, technical type business and the other is geared for heavier manufacturing operations. Both enjoy 24-hour truck and rail service and are within easy access to local, national, and international air service. Crawfordsville is uniquely positioned near the major markets of Chicago, Cincinnati, Louisville, St. Louis, and Indianapolis that are easily reached by the interstate highway system.

CRAWFORDSVILLE COMMERCE PARK

Located on the north side of Crawfordsville near Interstate 74, the Crawfordsville Commerce Park is a new, 325-acre, first-class development designed to create stimulating work environment in an attractive setting. The location offers opportunities for real estate developers and business owners looking for investment opportunities outside major metropolitan areas. This unique site offers advanced telecommunications and data services, municipal utilities, multiple development parcels, and outstanding accessibility.

NUCOR ROAD INDUSTRIAL CORRIDOR

Located strategically on Interstate 74 and US 32, this industrial corridor features centrally located transportation infrastructure to include interstate and rail transportation. Various parcel sizes are available and are serviced by excellent utilities and a regional sewer district. Access by air is nearby and major markets are within easy commuting distance. The corridor is home to several high-tech steel related industries such as Nucor Steel, Heidtman Steel, DuBose manufacturing, Performance Master and Whitesville Mill. These companies are known worldwide for providing quality employment opportunities and industry innovations.
