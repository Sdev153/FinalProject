Visitor Information

A NICE PLACE TO VISIT – A GREAT PLACE TO LIVE

Visitors to Crawfordsville and Montgomery County always find a warm and friendly welcome awaiting them when they arrive. Whether in the area to look for lost treasurers at our several antique stores, visiting one of our area’s historic downtowns, or just looking for a good meal, we are the community for you.


Having been voted one of the “100 Best Small Towns in America” *, Crawfordsville has continued to look to the future while maintaining the values of the past. There is “something for everyone” in Crawfordsville.


To get more information about different places to visit while in Crawfordsville, we recommend contacting the Montgomery County Visitor's Bureau by going to www.crawfordsville.org
